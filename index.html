Car Hire FAQs

Can I hire a car if I’m under 25 or over 75?

Yes, most car hire providers have a minimum age limit of 21. However, this may vary depending on the provider and destination. There may be additional charges if the main driver is under 25 or over 75 years old to cover the extra insurance cost. Any additional charge will be stated at the time of booking.

When will I be charged for optional extras?

You will be charged by the car hire provider when you pick up your car. You’ll need to pay in the local currency.

Which documents do I need to bring to the car hire desk?

The voucher we send you will contain the documents you need to bring. In most cases, you’ll need your:

  • Credit card in the main drivers name.
  • Driving licence – full licence held for at least one year.
  • Booking voucher

Do I need a credit card to book a hire car?

Yes you will require a credit card in the main drivers name. Most hire car providers will not accept cash, cheque, debit cards or a pre-paid travel card.

How much money do I need on my credit card?

You will need to have sufficient funds available on your credit card to the sum of the deposit excess amount for your hire car when you pick it up. This is standard practice for all car hire providers and will usually result in an amount being blocked on the main driver's credit card. The deposit amount will be detailed in the booking process and on your booking voucher.

Do I need to leave a deposit?

You will be required to a leave a deposit on your credit card when collecting your hire car. Customers wishing to not leave a deposit will be required to purchase additional insurance from the supplier at the rental desk.

Do I need additional insurance for my car hire?

When you book via Jet2holidays, we will usually include a Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) for you. This will cover certain types of damage to the vehicle. Even if you have a Collision Damage Waiver, car hire suppliers will ask you to buy their additional insurance at the rental desk to cover the deposit excess and other items not included in CDW. It is completely optional to take out their insurance, but the car hire supplier will need a credit card deposit if you don’t want to buy their cover.

Optional Damage Refund Insurance is available via the Jet2holidays booking confirmation page and within the Manage My Booking on Jet2holidays. If there is damage to the car or it is stolen, the car hire supplier will keep some or all of the excess to cover the costs, which you can claim back in full using the Damage Refund Insurance. Details on making a claim will be sent in your confirmation email.
